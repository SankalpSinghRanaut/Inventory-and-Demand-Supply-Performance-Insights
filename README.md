## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **5,000+ sales transactions** from a U.S.-based retail company to identify:

- 📈 Profitability trends
- 💰 Discount impact
- 🌎 Regional performance
- 🛒 Product performance
- 👥 Customer behavior
- 🚚 Shipping efficiency

The primary objective is to uncover hidden business problems and provide **data-driven recommendations** that improve profitability and operational efficiency.

---

# 🏢 Business Background

**SuperStore Inc.** is a U.S.-based retailer selling:

- 🪑 Furniture
- 💻 Technology
- 📝 Office Supplies

The company serves four major customer segments across multiple U.S. regions using several shipping methods.

# 🔍 Business Analysis

## 💰 Profitability Analysis

### Key Findings

- ❌ **21.1%** of all orders generated losses.
- 💸 Heavy discounting significantly reduced profitability.
- 📉 Low-margin products became unprofitable after discounts.

---

## 🌎 Regional Performance

| 🟢 East | Strong Profit |
| 🟡 Central | Average |

### 🟢 Top Performing Sub-Categories

- 📠 Copiers
- 📱 Phones
- 🎧 Accessories

### 🔴 Biggest Loss Makers

- 🪑 Tables (**-$17.7K**)
- 📚 Bookcases

### Most Profitable

- ⚡ Same Day

### Improvement Area

- 📦 Standard Class

Issues observed:

- Delivery delays

# 📊 Key Insights

## 📉 Profitability

- 21.1% of orders resulted in losses.
- Discounts were the primary cause.

---

## 💵 Pricing

- High discounts negatively impacted margins.
- Several products remained unprofitable regardless of sales volume.

---

## 🌎 Geography

- West and East dominated revenue.
- South region requires operational improvements.

---

## 👥 Customers

- Revenue is concentrated among a small number of loyal customers.
- Customer retention can significantly improve revenue.

---

## 🚚 Logistics

- Faster shipping correlated with better profitability.
- Standard Class requires optimization.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🎯 Pricing Strategy

- Reduce excessive discounting.
- Offer discounts only on high-margin products.

---

### 📦 Product Portfolio

- Reprice low-margin products.
- Bundle weak-performing items.
- Consider discontinuing consistently loss-making products.

---

### 🚚 Logistics

- Improve Standard Class delivery performance.
- Optimize shipping costs.

---

### 👥 Customer Strategy

- Launch loyalty programs.
- Re-engage inactive customers.
- Reward high-value customers.
